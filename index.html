About Me:

Hello! I am a PhD student studying Economics at Brown University. My research interests include Behavioral and Development Economics with a focus on social networks. Previously, I was an undergraduate research assistant for Raymond Fisman at Boston University, then a pre-doctoral fellow for Shing-Yi Wang and Susanna Berkouwer at Wharton's Business Economics and Public Policy Department.

I am also an award-winning independent filmmaker, having made feature-length films that have played in theaters and festivals in the U.S. and abroad.
